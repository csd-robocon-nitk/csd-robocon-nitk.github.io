"IDEAFACTORY is a collaborative space where young innovators transform cutting-edge research into impactful solutions for society and the nation."

The advanced manufacturing facilities at Ideafactory were instrumental in bringing our designs to life. Precision laser cutters allowed us to fabricate complex aluminium chassis structures and acrylic mounts directly from CAD — reducing iteration time dramatically. Extensive 3D printing gave us the freedom to prototype and refine specialised grippers, sensor mounts, and drive components across both robots without compromise.
